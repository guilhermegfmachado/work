About

Translator, teacher, and project coordinator based in Torres Vedras. I work across Portuguese and English — in cultural institutions, classrooms, production studios, and conservation sites. Master's in Translation (FCSH, Universidade Nova de Lisboa). C2 English (Cambridge). I've subtitled films, coordinated translation pipelines at TransPerfect, taught in secondary schools and language academies, covered contemporary performance for Público and QV, played guitar across Lisbon's music venues, built a geo-dome in Bulgaria, and sold organic produce at Príncipe Real on Saturday mornings.
